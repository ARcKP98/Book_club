Welcome to the Book Club Finder



We help you find a good reading club in Amsterdam.

Why Join Us

Student friendly

We are the only facilitator that has book clubs just for students. We know from personal experince that how difficult it is to find a book club that you can attend while managing university. We cater to student schedules and connect others who are in the same boat as you.

International community

Our club has tons of Internationals who come to Amsterdam to study at university. So you can be assured that language won't be a barrier when you join our club since everyone speaks English.

Exclusivity

To esnure you get the best experince, we focus on creating small groups of around 3-8 people. We believe in the notion of quality over quantity and have found that having intimate groups works better than having large groups. This ensures that no one is left out of discussions.

For Fun

Life is stressful and having a place where you can wind down with others who also share a love for reading is a nice way to de-stress. Not only can you read books and discuss them, you also get to socilaise with others and make connections for life. We also arrange meetups for all the groups atleast once every two months.

Great Selection

Not only can you get together with others to read books, we can also offer you great recommendations if you want to avoid decision decision fatigue. You don't have to choose the book we recommend and are more than welcome to pick the one your club agrees on but our current members love to use this suggestion since most people can be busy to make a decision. Get in touch to know more about this selection.

Intra group meetups

Once a month, we arrange a gathering for all our book clubs. This is a great opportunity to meet other book clubs in Amsterdam. We have different events every time there is a gethering, from trivia night to open mic nights. Location will be conveyed before the event.
